Mexican Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of refried beans
  • 1/2 cup of salsa
  • 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of green onions
  • 1/2 cup of black olives
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Spread the refried beans and salsa over the dough
  4. Sprinkle the cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese over the beans and salsa
  5. Top with the ground beef, tomatoes, green onions, and black olives
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes
  8. Top with sour cream before serving
