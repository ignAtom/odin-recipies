Pasta Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pasta
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of black olives
  • 1/2 cup of green olives
  • 1/2 cup of red onion
  • 1/2 cup of cucumber
  • 1/2 cup of feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of Italian dressing

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente
  2. Chop the cherry tomatoes, olives, red onion, and cucumber
  3. Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Drizzle the Italian dressing over the salad
  5. Toss the salad until everything is evenly coated
  6. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving
